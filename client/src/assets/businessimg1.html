Delivery challenges?
We can help.

Delivery can be overwhelming for operators without trusted logistics experience. Common challenges include capital constraints, inexperience with compliance, technology limitations, limited marketing resources etc.

greenrush Delivery offers best-in-class operational and last-mile logistics to support legal, safe, and profitable delivery to your customers. Our logistics professionals have built dozens of successful delivery companies (cannabis and mainstream) that now do $15M+ in monthly revenue.

Grow your own delivery and double your license value.

greenrush retail partners earn the majority revenue share of a new, high-volume operation while also growing and retaining their own delivery brand and customers.

greenrush will fund and manage your high-volume delivery operation at your location. Labor, inventory, vehicles, compliance, insurance, technology, marketing — delivery business in a box. You simply provide the license and location.

Whats included

In addition to the funding needed to establish and run the operation to positive cash-flow, we will also provide:

Delivery Infrastructure

  • Proxy Employees

    Recruit, interview, train and onboard delivery personel.

  • SOPs

    Design and implement delivery SOPs.

  • Delivery Technology

    Develop and implement delivery technology.

  • Vehicles & Insurance

    Secure delivery vehicles and insurance.

Product Inventory

  • Product Research

    Research local consumption trends and pricing.

  • Inventory Ordering

    Consult with negotiations and aquisitions of inventory.

  • Product Distribution

    Consult with negotiations and aquisitions of products to locations.

  • Online Menus

    Add inventory to greenrush, weedmaps, nugg, Leafly etc.

Delivery Execution

  • Customer Orders

    Customer orders online and is routed to applicable location.

  • Order is Prepared

    Inventory manager packs order and assigns to driver.

  • Dispatch Driver

    Central Dispatch routes order and Customer Care assists customer.

  • Deliver Order

    Driver parks vehicle, contacts customer and executes delivery.
